SolidGround

Objective

  • Place a rig and dig pipes.
  • Connect pipes to minerals to increase your $/sec and make money
  • Pipes cost more the further down you go
  • Magenta isn't worth as much at first. Refineries multiply its value.

Rig

Click to place on surface. Allows you to connect pipes.

Yellow outline

Connected to a pipe. Click to dig.

White outline

Connected to a pipe. Cannot dig until you make more money.

Magenta

Makes some money per second when connected to a pipe.

Cyan

Makes more money per second when connected to a pipe.
